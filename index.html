Where am I?

If you are reading this, chances are, you have made it to VolgaCTF 2021 Final. Congratulations!

What should I do?

It depends on whether your team is attending the event or playing remotely:

We are a remote team

You will get OpenVPN configs for your network by email and will be able to test network connection on 14th and 15th of September. The organizers will setup a vulnbox for you and will also provide you with an extra instance, which you can use to launch attacks and submit flags in order to reduce network latency between local and remote segments of the competition network.

You will be asked for your SSH public keys so that they could be set up on these instances.

On the big day, just connect to your team network, get access to your vulnbox and an extra instance. Play CTF!

We are not afraid of COVID playing onsite

Get a vulnbox, then launch it on one of your team's computers and connect it to the network via a bridged interface. Set static IP to 10.5.1XY.3 where XY stands for your team number (zero-based). Play CTF!

Your team number was made available to you by one of the volunteers.
